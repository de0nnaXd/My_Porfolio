My Projects

Project 1: Configuring A Remote Network Topology

Using BGP protocols, configured a branch topology from four separate WAN's that were all remotely managed from the Ontario Tech University Residence.
The Chicago Headquarters consisted of corporate management employees and manufactured the distribution of mining ASIC's.
The Vancouver Branch housed the server farm for both internal and external connections. From here administrative staff manage/monitor the servers to which they have direct contact.
The Toronto Branch was the primary location for the Sales department.
The Mexico Branch was responsible for research and development.

With this information, I created a way to pilot this topology, all from the residence of my school.
